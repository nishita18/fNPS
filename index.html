~ Social Studies Lab ~

NPS provides a very well-equipped social science lab consisting of numerous charts, still and working models. Students of different grades are taken to the lab regularly. Social science quizzes, exhibitions and group discussions are held at regular intervals, which enable the students to learn while doing. Social science lab provides a platform for students to share their presentations.Social Science Lab is a distinguishing feature of our school where children come and learn practically which helps them to think out of the box.

  • School's Annual Function

    The annual function of our school was held on 4 januvary. The Director of Education was the chief guest. He presided over the function and gave away the prizes. Preparations for the annual function were made a few days in advance. The school building was white-washed. The main hall was decorated. Those who were invited started coming for the function. The chief guest arrived. He was welcomed by the school Principal. He was garlanded. The Principal read out the Annual Report. The chief guest gave away prizes to brilliant students . He congratulated the Principal and staff on the progress made by the school. The Principal thanked the chief guest and the function came to a close with the singing of the National Anthem.
